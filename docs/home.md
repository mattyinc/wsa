# Learn the system before you risk your capital.

Wall Street Academy is a practical apprenticeship in capital markets, built from first principles. Read carefully, question assumptions, take notes, and build the judgment that investing demands.

## What you will learn

- How money and credit move through the financial system
- Why markets react to expectations, not simply headlines
- How analysts read businesses, risk, and valuation
- How investment decisions become portfolios

## Our philosophy

Finance is not a collection of shortcuts. It is a way of seeing systems, incentives, trade-offs, and uncertainty. WSA begins with understanding and earns its way toward investing.
